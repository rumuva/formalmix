A place where ancient techniques meet modern curiosity. We believe in the power of hands, the honesty of materials, and the rhythm of communal creation.

Nuestra Filosofía

Every stroke, every carve, every weave tells a story.

At Talleres Mixtos, we don't just teach skills; we cultivate a lineage. Our space is designed to be a living archive of human ingenuity, where the weight of a mallet or the texture of raw linen becomes a language of its own.

The 'Community' here is more than a list of members. It is a shared pulse of makers who understand that slow creation is the most radical act of the modern era. We invite you to lose yourself in the process and find yourself in the results.
